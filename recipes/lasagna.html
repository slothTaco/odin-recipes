Lasagne Zoodle Lasagne

One day, not having any gluten-free pasta on hand but lots of zucchini, I decided to use 'zoodles' instead of noodles.

The whole family enjoyed it! I don't use any salt in the recipe because the cheeses already contain it.

This dish is even better the next day and freezes well too.

Ingrediants

  • 4 zucchini
  • 1 1/2 cups of store-bought tomato sauce
  • 2/3 cup shredded mozzeralla cheese
  • 1 1/2 cups bechamel sauce
  • cup grated Parmigiano Reggiano cheese
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil; chopped

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (19o degrees C)
  2. Cut zucchini lengthwise into 1/4-inch thick slices with a knife
  3. Pour 2 tablespoons of tomato sauce on the bottom of a 9x13-inch backing dish
  4. Arrange zucchini slices in a single layer, slightly overlapping, over tomato sauce.
  5. Top with a thin layer of mozzarella, 1/3 of the bechamel, 1/3 tomato sauce, 1/3 of the parmigiano Reggiano cheese, and 1/3 of the basil
  6. Bake until sauce is bubbly and the top is golden brown; around 35 minutes
