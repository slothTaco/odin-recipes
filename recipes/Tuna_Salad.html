Tuna Salad

Learn how to make tuna salad with this recipe for a great dish when you are in a hurry.

It's very quick and easy to make and tastes great every time!

Ingrediants

  • 1 (5 ounce) can solid white tuna packed in water, drained
  • 1/4 cup creamy salad dressing
  • 1 tablespoon swet pickle relish, or to taste

steps

  1. gather all the Ingrediants
  2. mash tuna together with creamy salad drerssing and relish in a Small bowl
